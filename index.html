Meet the founder

Built on seventeen years
of personal trust.

JK Trade was founded by Mr. Ketan Rathod in 2008 from a single office on Tagore Road, Rajkot, a city that exports more diesel engines, brass parts and CNC components per square kilometre than most. What began as a single product line — silicone sealants to local workshops — grew into an authorised channel for eight global brands, because the customers kept asking.

We don’t manufacture. We do something arguably harder. We keep stock on the shelf, hold the technical know-how, and answer the phone at 9 PM on a Saturday when a glass facade in Surat is still on jack. Ketan still picks up himself.

“We don’t sell products. We supply the bond between an engineer’s drawing and what gets built. That’s a different responsibility.”
